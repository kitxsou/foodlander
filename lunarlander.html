Land your food in Noface's mouth!

How it works:

  • Controls: you can use W,A,S,D keys oder arrow keys to move up, left and right
  • Goal: land food into Noface's mouth while staying under a certain speed limit
  • Challenge: the higher the level, the faster and harder it gets to land the food, Noface's position changes with every level
  • Have fun!
